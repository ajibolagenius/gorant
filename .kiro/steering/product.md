# Product Overview

**Rant** is an anonymous social platform where users can express thoughts, frustrations, and emotions in a safe, supportive environment.

## Core Features
- **Anonymous Expression**: Users post rants without revealing identity, categorized by mood
- **Gamification System**: User levels, XP points, achievement badges, leaderboards, and challenges
- **Smart Organization**: Tag-based categorization, mood filtering, trending topics, advanced search
- **Community Interaction**: Like/comment system, bookmarking, sharing, real-time engagement
- **Modern UI/UX**: Responsive design, dark/light themes, masonry grid layout, smooth animations

## Key User Flows
- Post anonymous rants with mood selection
- Browse and filter content by mood, tags, or popularity
- Engage through likes, comments, and bookmarks
- Earn points and achievements through participation
- Discover trending content and challenges

## Target Audience
Users seeking a safe space for authentic self-expression without identity concerns, with gamification elements to encourage healthy community engagement.
