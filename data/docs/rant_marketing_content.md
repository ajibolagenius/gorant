## Product Hunt Launch Description

```
Rant is an open-source platform providing a safe, anonymous space for expressing your unfiltered thoughts.

Unlike traditional social media where your identity is tied to your content, Rant lets you share freely without accounts or personal information. Tag your posts with moods, see real-time updates, and connect with others experiencing similar emotions.

✅ Completely anonymous - no accounts needed
✅ Express your mood with visual mood tags
✅ Real-time community interaction
✅ Optimized for accessibility with high-contrast mode & reduced motion
✅ Keyboard shortcuts for power users
✅ Mobile-friendly responsive design

Rant is built with React, TypeScript, and Supabase, with privacy and accessibility as core values.

Join us in creating a more authentic online space where emotions can be expressed without fear of judgment.
```
